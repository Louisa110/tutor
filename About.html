#KnowUs

We envision a world where street musicians thrive beyond the streets, showcasing their talents on bigger stages, and contributing to meaningful live experiences that resonate with diverse audiences in events of all kinds.

Who we are?

Muzikant is a digital hub where the vibrant energy of street performances meets the sophistication of formal events. We bring street musicians into the spotlight, providing them with new opportunities to perform in curated spaces like cafes, weddings, and festivals. Our mission is to support local talent while simplifying the event planning process, ensuring that live music is accessible, high-quality, and perfectly suited to each occasion.

Muzikant aims to empower street musicians by providing them with professional opportunities and support. We connect talented performers with event spaces, ensuring that every musician has the chance to grow and every event has access to authentic live music. Through coaching, training, and performance opportunities, we strive to elevate the quality of live performances across all events.
